Grammar Feeder And Pool microservice

Common pool will be having below type of keywords

before, after, hour, min, day, days, months, month, year, years. Key words will map to either symbol or time in sec. like: before will map to '-' after will map to '+', and keyword like day will map to postfix annotation. i.e. 24 60 60 * *
